# English Book - Sports

## Suggested Title

**The Algorithmic Stadium: AI Agents, Fan Emotion, and the Future of Global Sport**

## Style

- Output type: `book`
- Language: `english`
- Domain: sport, media, analytics, AI agents

## Research Idea

This book would study how AI agents are changing sport beyond the scoreboard: tactical analysis, injury prevention, scouting, broadcast personalization, fan communities, ticket pricing, and live match storytelling. The central argument is that the modern stadium is becoming a data-rich social system where athletic performance, media, and emotion are orchestrated together.

## Creative Angle

The book can move from the pitch to the stands to the control room. It would compare human coaching intuition with agentic analysis, then ask whether sports become richer or more predictable when every moment is measured, simulated, and narrated by AI.
